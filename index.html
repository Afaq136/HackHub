About My Topic

Hackathons have become essential events in the tech industry, fostering innovation, creativity, and collaboration among participants. However, finding the right hackathon to participate in can be challenging, especially for newcomers. The Hackathon Hub aims to address this issue by providing a centralized platform where individuals can easily discover, join, and organize hackathons.

Data

One of the major challenges in hackathons is the lack of diversity among participants. Studies have shown that less than 15% of hackathon attendees are women, indicating a significant gender gap in the tech industry.
